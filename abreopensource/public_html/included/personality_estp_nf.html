You're An

ESTP!

"The Doer"

ESTPs are hands-on learners who live in the moment, seeking the best in life, wanting to share it with their friends. The ESTP is open to situations, able to improvise to bring about desired results.

One of an ESTPs greatest gifts if their ability to sense and perceive people's feelings, motivations, and attitudes. They are very vigilant and are able to discover what is on people's hearts beyond what their words express. If they sense that a person is in need, an ESTPs natural response is to do whatever they can to help, and do it now. They are known for their open-mindedness and because of this people tend to enjoy an ENTPs company. While they may be flexible, ESTPs do have a very strong sense of right and wrong and will stick to their principles very firmly. As an SP (Sensing-Perceiving) individual, they are great negotiator and are gifted with an ability to approach even the most testing situations with diplomacy.

ESTPs are active people who want to solve their problems rather than simply discuss them. They have a flexible and adaptable mentality and dynamic approach to solving problems, and people tend to feel more comfortable following the lead of an ESTP. As SP temperament loves action and is easily put off by the status quo. This coupled with their strong sense of right and wrong leads ESTPs to be the most active member of the community in terms of calling for justice. Because of their enthusiasm and high energy, ENTPs are happiest when they can serve in a quickly changing environment, such as crisis response, where their constant supply of energy is greatly needed. They have the ability to motivate others and their excitement about life and work can often be contagious. They are good at getting the ball rolling on a task, although they usually aren't the best in the outreach at following through with tasks every step of the way.

ESTPs are likely to enjoy service areas and callings where they can:
Enjoy spontaneous interactions with different types of people
Contribute their ability to absorb and retain facts
Apply their life experience to solving problems
Take risks in a fast-paced environment
Work free from rules and rigid restrictions
Help in practical ways that deal with people and things, producing a tangible result
Be organized according to their own standards
Take logical approaches to solving a problem
